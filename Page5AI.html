AI: Spade Coco

These images were taken in San Francisco as well as San Jose. Over the summer of 2021, I was able to visit these places with my family, hence their sentimentality and meaning to me. As someone who appreciates the Impressionist movement, at which Claude Monet was in front of, using AI to make my photographs similar to the work of Monet was enjoyable and a way to capture the beauty of life and the theme of family in one project.
